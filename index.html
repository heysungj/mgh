Become a member of a world class cancer center  

The Mass General Cancer Center’s Department of Radiation Oncology is a leader in developing and implementing some of the most innovative and effective radiation therapies in the world. Our medical dosimetrists work with internationally recognized oncology specialists as part of a National Cancer Institute Comprehensive Cancer Center, including the only proton therapy site in New England. Mass General Cancer Center provides patients with access to the latest treatments and technologies for all types of cancers with exceptional team-based care. 

We look forward to welcoming new teammates to help deliver exceptional, personalized, and comprehensive cancer care. As part of our team, you’re afforded lots of potential.

Why Boston?

 

Boston's reputation as the birthplace of freedom has made it a place where diverse communities flourish.  While the city is recognized as a center for world-class healthcare, there are countless ways to keep yourself entertained here, whether you enjoy museums, shopping, theatre-going and eating at world class restaurants, or attending sporting events, concert, comedy shows, or special events.

The Mass General difference

Massachusetts General Hospital was established to provide care to Boston's sick, regardless of socioeconomic status. We have remained at the forefront of medicine by fostering a culture of collaboration and education, pushing the boundaries of medical research, and maintaining an unwavering commitment to the diverse community we were created to serve.  As Boston’s largest city hospital, our priority is the well-being of our patients—near and far. As a team we can maintain a singular focus on providing the highest quality, most compassionate care to every patient.
